My background

I returned to college to collaborate on AI projects with my former professor, aiming to deepen my knowledge in this area. I subsequently took on a position in the lab at NCTU, where I worked on data pipelines and collected data from real-world sources. During this time, I gained experience with Python and Pandas.

In 2021, I was accepted into the Computer Science program at Arizona State University and completed my degree in 2024. Most of the courses I took for my master's were focused on AI-related fields. I particularly enjoyed the AI classes and also completed several relevant database courses, which helped me manage and process data during the training phase.
